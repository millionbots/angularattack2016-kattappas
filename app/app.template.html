GadgetGeeks

This application aims at using the interest and kinda close obession of technology geeks to provide useful info to everyone in the context of buying latest gadgets.

We are very much aware of how much these geeks want to discuss, review and provide feedbacks about the technology.

Here we are tryng to provide a platform for them to provide info and others can get fair idea to buy products. And based on the users feedback about the review, the reviewers are rated.
